Evallish Global Experience

Practical and Executable Solutions to Support Business Growth

For over 17 years, Evallish BPO has been providing strategic support to businesses across various industries, helping them grow and thrive through tailored solutions that drive efficiency and optimize operations. We offer practical, executable solutions designed to meet the unique needs of our clients, focusing on key areas of business consulting:

Our Call Center Consulting Services

1. Operations Consulting

  • Gap Analysis: Identifying areas for improvement and providing actionable solutions.
  • Quick Strike Assessments: Fast, targeted evaluations to address immediate operational challenges.
  • Process Mapping: Analyzing and redesigning workflows to streamline operations.
  • Customer Journey Mapping: Visualizing and improving customer touchpoints for a seamless experience.
  • Department Redesigns: Restructuring teams and workflows for optimal performance.

2. HR Consulting

  • Compensation and Benefit Structuring: Creating competitive compensation packages.
  • KPIs & Performance Management: Defining key performance indicators and implementing tracking systems.
  • Performance Improvement Plans: Enhancing individual and team performance.
  • Motivation and Retention Plans: Improving employee engagement and reducing turnover.
  • Change Management: Managing transitions smoothly.
  • Organizational Structuring: Optimizing team hierarchies for efficiency.

3. Training

  • Training Needs Analysis: Assessing training gaps and developing personalized programs.
  • Training Program Audits: Reviewing programs for effectiveness and alignment with goals.
  • Trainer Certifications: Certifying trainers to ensure high-quality instruction.
  • Training Methodology & Curriculum Reviews: Evaluating and enhancing learning outcomes.
  • Virtual Training Redesigns: Updating virtual training programs for modern needs.

4. Compliance Consulting

  • Regulatory Compliance: Adhering to industry standards and regulations.
  • HR Compliance: Ensuring all HR practices align with laws and best practices.
  • PCI DSS, TCPA, HIPAA Compliance: Providing guidance on privacy, security, and consumer protection.

5. Business Growth Consulting

  • New Market Entry: Expanding into new geographic regions and industries.
  • Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A): Supporting due diligence and integration processes.
  • Business Development: Assisting with lead generation and market expansion strategies.
  • Business Planning: Crafting take-to-market strategies for success.

6. Call Center Technology Integration

  • CRM Integration: Integrating CRM systems with call center operations.
  • AI and Automation: Introducing tools to streamline processes and enhance experiences.
  • Omnichannel Support: Implementing systems for seamless communication across various channels.

These services are tailored to help businesses optimize their call center operations, improve customer satisfaction, and drive growth. At Evallish BPO, we are committed to delivering cost-effective, high-performance solutions that meet the unique needs of each client.
